This Game is about you, as a vlogger, going into the forest. With no interesting stories happening in the town, you decide to go to the forest.

As you enter, you notice car tracks on the dirt, ahead with two signs. One points to a shadow of a house and the other to a car. You decide to go to the Car first.

You find a note with a code in it, interesting enough, you find an abandoned safe in the house.
